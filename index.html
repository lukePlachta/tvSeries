Breaking Bad

First aired 2008
Number of series 5
Number of episodes 62
Certificate 15
Genre Action, Drama, Thriller

When a mild mannered Chemistry Teacher named Walter White is diagnosed with terminal lung cancer and told he only has two years to live. He is worried about how his family will cope financially but then is amazed to find out how much money a couple of meth dealers have made when they have been caught. Then when a former student of his (Jesse Pinkman) is almost arrested for drug dealing, Walter forms a partnership with Jesse where they cook and deal crystal meth but in a cautious manor which proves far easier said than done. Jesse not only deals drugs but he takes them as well, Walter has to battle his deteriorating health, try and keep his job going and hide any evidence of his drug producing from his family. He will never be far away from the investigation with his brother-in-law Hank being an agent for the Drugs Enforcement Agency.

To help keep their operation a secret Walter hires a shady lawyer named Saul who uses his agent Mike to help clean up whenever Walter and Jesse run into trouble and in exchange Saul takes a percentage of their intake. He also helps them form a partnership with the owner of a chicken shop. The chicken shop turns out to be the front of the biggest meth lab in the area run by a man named Gus Fring. Fring is very cautious over who he does business with although is involved in a drug war with the Cartel.

All the money Walter has made has made his wife Skyler suspicious and eventually she figures out what he has been doing which adds to Walter's problems. He makes her promise not to tell Hank and Skyler ends up helping Walter cover his tracks by buying a car wash. Hoping to launder the drug money in their new business. However as time progresses Hank and his DEA collegues manage to figure out the truth but Walter has become so powerful will there be anything Hank can do about it.

Shameless

First aired 2004
Number of series 11
Number of episodes 139
Certificate 18
Genre Drama, Comedy

The lives of a few families living in a council estate area in Manchester. Starting with the Gallagher's, the dad Frank is an acloholic waster who spends most of his time going awol, getting drunk and waking up in random places. He has spent next to no time in helping raise his six children, grows to 10 by the end of the entire series. His eldest daughter Fiona has the task of raising the kids as their mum keeps on walking out on them. She comes back every now and then, has another baby with Frank before disappearing again and without the child.

The other children do get more and more independant as time goes on especially as Fiona moves away. Frank temporarily has another wife in Sheila who he cheats on with her own daughter who was going out with his eldest son Lip. After realising what Frank is like Sheila leaves him and takes the children with her. Lip does end up getting his next girlfriend Mandy Maguire pregnant, a very dangerous move as she comes from a very dangerous family led by the feared Paddy Maguire. The Maguire's do become more apart of the show as the series continues and even welcome back their eldest son Jamie who was serving 10 years in prison for murder. But does he come back entirely the same. Paddy and his wife Mimi do eventually get divorced after Paddy's past finally catches up with him only for Mimi to then marry a teenager after he accidentally gets her pregnant to the dismay of all her grown up children.

The Maguire's next son Micky as well as Ian one of the Gallagher's both realise they are gay but are unable to come out to their families as they fear they will not be accepted. However some people do find out but promise not to tell anyone. Ian ends up going out with his boss Kash who is actually married to a woman Yvonne and they have two kids in Menna and Chesney, Kash and Yvonne run a corner shop which Chesney takes over in the future. The running of the shop comes into question when Yvonne finds out her husband's secret and throws him out and then moves abroad with her partner Stan who is a police officer.

It is a frustrating time for the police especially the ambitious Carrie who cannot seem to find any evidence to put the Maguire's or any of the Gallagher's away as the families are clever like that. Then her partner finds out that Carrie has been using her dead sister's profile to become a police officer.

This is Us

First aired 2016
Number of series 1
Number of episodes 18
Certificate 12
Genre Drama

The story of a family but is set in two times. The earlier time when Jack and Rebecca Pearson become parents to triplets but the later time when all the kids have grown up, one now with a family and the others still figuring out what to do.

When giving birth to the triplets there was a complication and sadly one of the babies died. However at the hospital Jack finds out from a fireman who has dropped off an abandoned baby he found at his fire station. With no known family and feeling the loss of one of the babies both Jack and Rebecca agree to adopt Randall and raise him as one of there own. Despite being raised as a Pearson Randall very early on finds that he is quite different to the rest of the family and sometimes feels he has to over try to impress in an attempt to fit in.

In the future Randall does however track down his biological father William, is a bit reluctant to talk to him and forgive him but does allow William to meet his wife and children. Sadly for Randall he finds out soon after his father has terminal cancer and does not have much time to reconnect with him.

Kevin the other brother is trying to make it as an actor as is playing a role known as The Manny. He is a celebrity but does lose his way after a public outburst causing him to lose his job, he then moves to New York and tries to make it as a stage actor. Both him and Randall do also try to repair their relationship as they have been at loggerheads for years.

Kate the sister is trying to lose weight, she does not get on that well with her mum or new step dad, she was however quite close to her father Jack who is now dead. Kate also has his aches on her fireplace. She does however have a new man in her life who is also trying to lose weight as well.

Rebecca following the death of Jack is actually now married to his best friend Miguel, the exact cause of Jack's death is still unknown but from what we do know, him and Rebecca were taking a trial seperation after Jack got jealous of Rebecca following a music career, he also believed one of her co stars was trying to steal her.
